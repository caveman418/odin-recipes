Scrambled Egg Recipe

Description

Can't go wrong with some simple scrambled eggs when you're feeling lazy.

Ingredients

  • A couple eggs
  • Butter or pam spray
  • Salt and pepper maybe

Directions

  1. Melt butter on pan
  2. Break eggs into pan then break up the yolks
  3. Add seasoning or add in peppers/onions/whatever to your liking
  4. Once solid and chunky, turn off heat and enjoy
